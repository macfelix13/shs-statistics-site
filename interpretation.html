Reading Data

Tables and graphs are powerful tools for organizing and presenting data. Students should learn to:

  • Identify trends and patterns in bar charts and line graphs
  • Compare values across categories in tables
  • Interpret pie charts to understand proportions

Making Predictions

Data trends help us forecast future outcomes. Students should be able to:

  • Use line graphs to predict future values
  • Understand correlation and causation
  • Apply data interpretation in real-life scenarios like budgeting or planning
